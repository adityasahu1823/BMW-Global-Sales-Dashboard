## BMW Global Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

### Business Problem
The objective of this project was to analyze BMW’s global sales performance across regions and product categories to identify growth trends, underperforming segments, and opportunities for strategic decision-making.

### Approach
- Cleaned and structured sales data for consistency and analysis  
- Built a data model to analyze performance across regions, products, and time  
- Created DAX measures for KPIs such as total revenue, YoY growth, and sales distribution  
- Designed a multi-page dashboard with executive-level views for quick decision-making  

### Key Insights
- Certain regions consistently contributed the majority of total revenue, highlighting geographic dependency  
- YoY growth analysis revealed both high-growth markets and declining regions needing attention  
- A small number of product categories drove a large share of overall sales, indicating concentration risk  
- Seasonal trends impacted sales performance across different markets  

### Impact
This analysis can help business stakeholders:
- Focus on high-performing regions and replicate successful strategies  
- Identify underperforming markets and take corrective actions  
- Optimize product portfolio and marketing investments  
- Support data-driven strategic planning  

### Tools Used
- Power BI (Dashboard Development, DAX, Data Modeling)  
- Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)  

### Features
- KPI tracking (Revenue, YoY Growth, Regional Performance)  
- Interactive filters for region, product category, and time  
- Executive-level dashboard design for quick insights
